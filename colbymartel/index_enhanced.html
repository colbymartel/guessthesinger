About Me

Passionate content creator and entrepreneur building the future of entertainment

Building Digital Empires

With over 500K YouTube subscribers and 200K TikTok followers, I've built a community that spans across multiple platforms. My content focuses on music, entertainment, and lifestyle, creating authentic connections with audiences worldwide.

As the founder of GuessTheSinger.com, I've transformed a simple music guessing game into a comprehensive entertainment platform featuring live shows, digital games, and interactive experiences that bring people together through the universal language of music.

My journey has taken me to 27 colleges across the country, where I've had the privilege of connecting with students and sharing my passion for entertainment and entrepreneurship.
