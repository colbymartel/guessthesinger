Yes — we offer a free pilot option this semester with a $500 prize pool, or discounted first-time shows.

Our format is uplifting, team-based, and inclusive — a fun break from comparison-driven social feeds.

Absolutely. Magic and comedy collabs are common at NACA; we’re open to smart pairings.
