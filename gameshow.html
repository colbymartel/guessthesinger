About

Guess The Singer is the ultimate music trivia showdown where pop culture meets high-energy gameplay... hosted by YouTube creator Colby Martel (500,000+ subscribers)! Each round students will be challenged to guess a mystery music artist by asking strategic questions, unlocking clues, and avoiding elimination from the iconic Wheel of Fate.

Students will begin by selecting from a pool of lanyards that feature various music artists which each correspond to a capsule inside a giant gumball machine, which is used to randomly select participants throughout the game. Initial participants of each round will be selected at random and by skill through brief trivia sessions. The first student to guess the singer each round will win a cash prize from $100 to $5,000 depending on the package selected.
