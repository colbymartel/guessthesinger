About The Game

Everyday until Christmas, there will be a new artist to guess and a new mystery prize to win! Anyone who correctly guesses the daily artist and enters the Google Form will have a random and equal chance to win the prize of the day. A new prize will be announced each day and there will be one winner each day. Grand prizes for this month include AirPods Max, Concert Tickets, and more!

How To Play

  • Select yes/no questions to figure out the mystery artist.
  • Try to minimize the number of questions and hints you use!
  • Everyday until Christmas will feature a new artist to guess.
  • Have fun, share your results with friends, and enter to win prizes!
